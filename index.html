    Methodology and disclaimer

    This site is a trivia game based on public records and reputable reporting. Questions are labeled by type (conviction, court filing, investigation, allegation, etc.). A lawsuit, report, or allegation is not proof of wrongdoing unless a court finding or conviction is cited. Sources are provided for every question so you can verify context.
